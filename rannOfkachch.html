The Rann of Kachch Dispute (1965)

  • Rann of Kachch lies between Sindh (Pakistan) and Gujarat (India).
  • After 1947, both claimed this salt desert region.
  • In 1965, small border clashes turned into armed conflict.
  • Ceasefire reached through British mediation.
  • In 1968, tribunal awarded most area to India, rest to Pakistan.
