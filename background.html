Background Details of the 1965 War

  • Post-partition tensions remained high between India and Pakistan.
  • Both nations claimed the princely state of Jammu and Kashmir.
  • Skirmishes and infiltration incidents increased during the 1960s.
  • Pakistan’s growing military confidence encouraged confrontation.
  • India strengthened its defenses after the 1962 war with China.
