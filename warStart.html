Who Started the 1965 War?

  • The conflict began when Pakistan launched Operation Gibraltar in August 1965.
  • Pakistani troops and infiltrators entered Indian-administered Kashmir to start an uprising.
  • India responded by crossing the Line of Control and capturing strategic areas.
  • Skirmishes turned into a full-scale war by September 1965.
  • Both nations blamed each other, but most historians agree Pakistan initiated the conflict.
