Course of the 1965 War

  • War began on 6 September 1965 when India attacked Lahore.
  • Pakistan countered with Operation Grand Slam in Jammu sector.
  • Major battles were fought at Lahore, Sialkot, and Thar-Rajisthan.
  • Pakistani troops defended the BRB Canal heroically, halting Indian advance.
  • Both nations used air and tank forces on a large scale.
  • After 17 days of intense fighting, the UN intervened for peace.
  • Ceasefire was accepted on 23 September 1965.
