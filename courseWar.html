Course of the 1965 War

  • War began on 6 September 1965 when India attacked Lahore.
  • Pakistan countered with Operation Grand Slam in Jammu sector.
  • Major battles fought at Lahore, Sialkot and Chhamb–Jaurian.
  • Pakistani forces defended BRB Canal and halted Indian advance.
  • Both sides used aircraft and tanks on a large scale.
  • After 17 days of fighting, UN called for ceasefire.
  • Ceasefire accepted on 23 September 1965.
