Key Leaders of the 1965 War

  • Field Marshal Ayub Khan — President of Pakistan, directed overall war policy and defense strategy.
  • Lal Bahadur Shastri — Prime Minister of India, led the country with firm resolve during wartime.
  • Zulfikar Ali Bhutto — Pakistan’s Foreign Minister, played a key diplomatic and political role.
  • Aziz Ahmed — Pakistan’s Foreign Secretary, major contributor in Tashkent negotiations.
